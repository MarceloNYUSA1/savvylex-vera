## About
Vera is SavvyLex's Claude for Law interface — a configuration and delivery layer for Anthropic's Claude models, purpose-built for tax law boutiques, in-house legal teams, and law schools.

- **BYOK Architecture** — clients bring their own Anthropic Enterprise key
- **ZDR Required** — Zero Data Retention on all production deployments
- **Audit Trail** — immutable per-matter logs for privilege and bar compliance
- **LexAgents** — agentic legal workflows with attorney-in-the-loop gates
